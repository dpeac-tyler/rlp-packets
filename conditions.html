Question Logic

This section lets you add follow-up questions for the Yes/No and Either/Or questions you created in the Question Builder. These questions are based on conditions or logic, and they will show or remain hidden depending on the user's answer.

For example: If you ask the question, "Do you hold a license in another state?," the user must answer Yes or No. You can add a follow-on question, such as "List the state(s) in which you currently hold a license," which will only appear if the answer to the first question was Yes.

NOTE: If you don't need to include question logic in your application, click Skip to move to the next section.

Yes/No; Either/Or

Choose this type when only a Yes or No response is required, or when you need the user to choose between two options.

Yes/No Example: Do you currently hold a specialty?

Either/Or Example: Do you plan to operate as Owner or as Manager?
